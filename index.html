About the Solar System

The Solar System consists of the Sun, eight planets, their moons, and many other objects such as asteroids and comets. It is an incredible part of our universe and continues to fascinate scientists and space enthusiasts around the world.

Interesting Facts

  • The Sun contains more than 99% of the Solar System's mass.
  • Jupiter is the largest planet in our Solar System.
  • Saturn is famous for its beautiful rings made of ice and rock.
